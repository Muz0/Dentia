Sinus Lift

  • Home Page
  • Sinus Lift

Surgical procedure to augment the upper jaw

Sinus lift is a surgical procedure aimed at increasing the bone volume in the upper jaw for implant placement. This operation expands the bone area beneath the sinus and creates a solid foundation for the implant.

Main Types

Two methods of sinus lift

Open Sinus Lift

Lifting the sinus membrane using an open method and placing a bone graft.

Closed Sinus Lift

Increasing bone volume with a minimally invasive method through small access points.

Sinus lift is an important and effective solution for successful implant treatment.
